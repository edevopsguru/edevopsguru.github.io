Who is eligible for this program?

This program is open for working professionals in software industry with 1 to 10 year experience. You should have basic understanding of software development lifecycle, OS, Programming or Scripting Concepts and atleast 1 year of work (job) experience. You should be eager to succeed with an attitude to learn new skills. We welcome professionals from Testing, Release management and Operations background who want to build career in DevOps.

I dont have programming experience, can I join?

Cource expects basic scripting or programming concepts. We encourage you to prepare before your entrance test through self-learning. Our course advisor will be happy to share free on-line resources to help you prepare. In absence of these skills, you will not be able to get the best out of the course.

What is salary share agreement?

This plan oferred to candidates based out of India on risk-reward model. eDevOpsGuru carries the risk of placement here. If you clear the entrance test, you need to enter into a "Salary Share Agreement" with eDevOpsGuru. You pay 15% of your monthly gross base salary for a period of 2 years. This is payable only if your annual CTC is above the promised Min CTC at the time of enrolment. Once you get the job, you pay 15% of your CTC on montly basis to eDevOpsGuru.com for a period of 2 years or till you have paid INR 7,00,000, whichever is earlier.

What if I dont't get a job?

Our coaches will continue to support you for the 6 month from the program start date / 2 months from program complete. If you still do not succeed in getting a job you are not not liable to pay under SSA Plan. If you are enrolled in Zer-Risk plan, we will refund your full fee. Our liability is limited to refund of fee.
