Why collaborative learning?

Collaborative learning is a great way to build skills and enhance qualification along with your present jobs. You can do it from the comfort of your own home while engaging with your peer and coaches in real-time. At the same time, one should remember, you get out what you put in, so even collaborative learning requires dedication. Its real people, live engaging with you in real-time. It's very satisfying to help someone with a tricky question at the same time easy to get help on a concept you are struggling to grasp.

Limitations of Self Study / Online programs

All popular models of online training like CBTs, online tutorials, even books etc. rely on the student's self monitoring during the study process. It is proven by research that in self learning or distance learning programes, the program completion rate is poor. One reason for this is that the training provider loses touch with the student which leads to students losing interest in the program. The missing point is the presence of a mentor like in a classroom. If one is able to provide a mentor who is always available to answer student's minutest of queries, perform assessments regularly, evaluate examination and projects, then the drop out rate can be cut drastically.
